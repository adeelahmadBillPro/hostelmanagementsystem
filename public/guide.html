Welcome to HostelHub

HostelHub is a complete hostel management platform that helps hostel owners manage rooms, residents, billing, food, staff, and day-to-day operations from a single dashboard.

Whether you run a small private hostel or a chain of university hostels, HostelHub scales to meet your needs with multi-hostel support, role-based access, and comprehensive reporting.

What HostelHub Manages: Rooms & Beds, Residents, Monthly Billing, Food Menu & Orders, Staff & Salaries, Expenses, Visitors, Complaints, Gate Passes, Messaging, and more.

User Roles & Access

HostelHub has 4 main user roles, each with specific permissions:

Super Admin

Platform Administrator

Manages the entire platform. Controls which hostel owners can use the system, sets subscription plans, and views platform-wide analytics and revenue.

Hostel Owner (Tenant Admin)

Hostel Owner / Organization

Owns one or more hostels. Has full access to manage buildings, rooms, residents, billing, food, staff, expenses, and reports. Can hire managers and assign them to specific hostels.

Hostel Manager

Day-to-Day Operations

Assigned to specific hostel(s) by the owner. Manages residents, records payments, handles food menu, responds to complaints and messages. Cannot access other hostels.

Resident

Hostel Resident (Boy/Girl)

Has a personal portal to view bills, order food, send messages to management, file complaints, apply for gate passes, and see hostel facilities.

Getting Started (Hostel Owner)

1
Create Account
Register on the platform with your name, email, and phone number.
2
Add Your Hostel
Enter hostel name, type (Private/University/Government), address, and contact details.
3
Set Up Infrastructure
Add buildings (Block A, Block B), floors per building, then rooms with bed count and rent per bed. Beds are created automatically.
4
Add Residents
Register each resident with personal info, CNIC, and assign them to a specific room and bed. Set advance and security deposit amounts.
5
Set Up Food Menu
Add breakfast, lunch, dinner items with prices. Mark which items are free (e.g., roti with meal). Set available days.
6
Generate Monthly Bills
At month end, click "Generate Bills" to automatically create bills for all residents including rent, food charges, parking, and electricity.
7
Collect Payments
Record payments when residents pay via Cash, Bank, JazzCash, or EasyPaisa. Or let residents upload payment proof from their portal.

Billing & Payments

How Monthly Bills Work

When you generate bills, the system automatically calculates each resident's charges:

Charge TypeHow It's Calculated
Room RentBased on the bed's monthly rate (set when room was created)
Food ChargesSum of all food orders placed during the month
Parking FeeMonthly fee if resident has registered vehicle
ElectricityFrom meter reading (units × rate per unit)
Previous BalanceUnpaid amount from last month (auto carried forward)
Advance DeductionAmount deducted from advance paid at move-in
DiscountManager can apply discount with reason

Payment Methods

MethodProcess
CashManager records payment directly
Bank TransferResident transfers to hostel account, manager records with reference #
JazzCashMobile payment - resident uploads screenshot as proof
EasyPaisaMobile payment - resident uploads screenshot as proof

Payment Proof System

1
Resident pays via JazzCash/Bank and takes a screenshot
2
Resident uploads the screenshot in "Pay Bill" portal page
3
Manager reviews the proof in "Payment Proofs" page
4
Manager approves → bill automatically marked as paid

Partial Payments

Residents can pay in installments. Each payment reduces the balance. The remaining amount carries forward to the next month.

Bill Disputes

If a resident believes a charge is incorrect, they can file a dispute from their portal. The manager reviews and adjusts the bill if needed.

Food Menu & Ordering

Manager: Setting Up the Menu

  • Add items by meal type: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack
  • Set price per item in PKR
  • Mark items as FREE (e.g., roti with meal)
  • Set free quantity limits (e.g., 3 roti free, 4th onwards PKR 10)
  • Choose available days (Mon-Sun)
  • Set maximum order quantity per item

Resident: Ordering Food

  • Open "Food Menu" in portal → see today's available items
  • Use +/- buttons to select quantity
  • Total calculates live as you change quantities
  • Free items show "FREE" badge (e.g., roti with biryani)
  • Review order summary before confirming
  • Food charges automatically added to monthly bill

Resident Portal

Every resident gets a personal portal with these features:

Dashboard

See room/bed info, current month's bill summary, recent notices, and quick action links.

Hostel Info

View hostel profile, facilities available (Wi-Fi, AC, parking, etc.), and contact details.

My Bill

Full itemized breakdown of monthly bill. See every food order, room rent, parking, electricity charges. File disputes if something looks wrong.

Pay Bill

Upload payment proof (screenshot of bank/JazzCash/EasyPaisa transaction). Track approval status.

Food Menu

Order from today's menu with live price calculator. See free items, quantity controls, and order summary.

Messages

Chat directly with hostel management. Create topic-based conversations. Send text, images, and documents.

Complaints

File complaints (AC broken, water issue, etc.) and track resolution status.

Gate Pass

Apply for leave with dates and reason. Track approval from management.

Notices

Read hostel announcements and rules posted by management.

Profile Settings

Upload photo (optional), update phone, emergency contact, blood group. Change password.

Additional Features

Hostel Amenities Profile

Manager can set which facilities are available in the hostel. 60+ pre-loaded options across 6 categories: Hostel General, Room, Washroom, Dining, Security, and Transport/Nearby. Just toggle on/off. Residents see the available facilities.

Visitor Management

Record every visitor with name, CNIC, phone, purpose, which resident they're visiting, and time in/out.

Expense Tracking

Track all hostel expenses by category (Gas, Electricity, Internet, Water, Maintenance, Staff Salary, etc.). View monthly P&L summary with charts showing income vs expenses.

Reports & Analytics

Interactive charts and reports: Occupancy rates, revenue trends, expense breakdowns, resident lists, and payment summaries. All exportable to Excel.

Notifications

Bell icon in the header shows unread count. Click to see: unread messages, unpaid bills, pending payment proofs, new complaints, and gate pass requests.

Messaging

Built-in chat system between residents and management. Topic-based conversations with file attachment support (images, PDFs, videos).

Bulk Operations

  • Generate bills for all residents at once
  • Mark multiple payments as paid
  • Bulk checkout at end of semester
  • Import residents from Excel spreadsheet
  • Import rooms from Excel
  • Export any report to Excel
  • Enter meter readings for all rooms on one page

Dark Mode

Full dark mode support across all pages. Toggle with the moon/sun icon in the header.
